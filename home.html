A home to your buddy

To feel at home means to be comfortable in a place,and at Chetty's Haven we provide that look and feel of a home to your pet. We are here to help your pet stay happy and healthy.

OUR SERVICES

Feeding

Feeding is done at the same time each day to facilitate and regulate their digestion. Special diet is given to pets with special needs.

Grooming

Cleaning is done regulary and according to your preference. Paw nails are cut weekly while hair/fur is blushed regulary. Your pet is entitled to a clean environment throughout their stay at our Pet Haven

Walk & Play

Taking a pet for a walk or play provides mental stimulation and physical exercize. Here at Chetty's Haven we spend an amble time walking and playing with your pet depending on their age, breed, health and size since our yard is spacious.

Training

Chetty's Haven offers training services where we train your pet to react to particular cues and commands

Vet services

At our Pet Haven, we pride ourselves at serving your pets with personalised vetcare. Good health brings happy lives to your pets.

Safety

A haven is a place of safety and our environment is a peaceful and secure for your buddies.

WHO ARE WE?

about Chetty's Petcare

Chetty's Haven is a petcare centre where we care and take responsibilities of pets while the owners are away. We have professional and passionate veterinary, trainers, kennel attendants, groomers, pet caretakers and sitters. Our team work together to ensure quality services are given to pets for their safety and wellbeing. Our facility is designed to accomodate dogs, cats, rabbits, fish, guinea pigs, horses, birds, reptiles and exotic pets. Pet adoption is also available, our Haven is full of happy, healthy and house-trained pets just waiting for someone to bring them home. In cooperation with our clients we are able to rehome and rehabilitate pets involved in cruelty, abuse or are stray. The love we have for pets give us unwavering commitment to take care of them.
